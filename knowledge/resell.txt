How to Avoid Listings Being Taken Down
Tips to Prevent Your Listings from Being Removed or Banned on Marketplaces
Account Age: Let your eBay account mature before listing commonly replicated products. New accounts listing such items can raise suspicion and increase the likelihood of being flagged. Established accounts are less prone to such scrutiny.,
Product Listings: Avoid including serial numbers in your listings, as some suppliers reuse serials that can be identified. Ensure packaging authenticity; for example, genuine Apple AirPods don't come wrapped in plastic due to their eco-friendly packaging. Including such details in photos could lead to your listings being removed and raise buyer concerns.
Sell Genuine Products: Begin by selling authentic products before introducing replicas. Having a mix of genuine items boosts your credibility, even when you start selling replicas.,
Optimize Your Profile: Present yourself as a trustworthy seller with a well-crafted profile. Use a clear profile picture and provide detailed, accurate product descriptions. A professional profile enhances your legitimacy and trustworthiness.,
By following these recommendations, you can reduce the risk of your listings being removed and establish yourself as a reliable seller in the online marketplace.
